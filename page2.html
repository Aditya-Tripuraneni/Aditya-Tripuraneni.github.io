More about this project

Our group was tasked with designing a wind turbine that supplies electric power for all of Kingston, Ontario. As a large hydro company has contacted us, the design of this wind turbine will lead to a farm of wind turbines being built located on Wolfe Island. The goal is to design an efficient wind turbine maximizing the production of electricity by minimizing the inertia of the blade. In addition, ensuring a long life span is critical to remain a reliable source of electric power to Kingston, Ontario.

More about this project

Get A Grip consists of a team divided into two sub-teams, a modelling team and a computing team. During this project, the team had to design a container to safely transport a medical surgical tool to a designated autoclave for sterilization. The modelling team was tasked with creating a container that fits within the dimensions of the autoclave and could securely contain the surgical tool restricting movement. The computing team designed code to control a q-arm which allowed for the transportation of the container to the autoclave. During this project, many variables needed to be accounted for due to the tight constraints of ensuring the mass of the container did not exceed 350g and 3D print time remained under two hours.
