More about this project

Our group was tasked with designing a wind turbine that supplies electric power for all of Kingston, Ontario. As a large hydro company has contacted us, the design of this wind turbine will lead to a farm of wind turbines being built located on Wolfe Island. The goal is to design an efficient wind turbine maximizing the production of electricity by minimizing the inertia of the blade. In addition, ensuring a long life span is critical to remain a reliable source of electric power to Kingston, Ontario.

This project taught our team a lot about the importance of communication, problem-solving and the engineering process behind designing projects and letting ideas come to life
